    <h1>Laporan Analisis Matriks</h1>
    <p>Tugas: Perhitungan Determinan (Ekspansi Baris) dan Invers (Adjoin)</p>

    <h2>BAGIAN A: Determinan (Ekspansi Baris)</h2>

    <div class="soal">
        <h3>1. Matriks 2x2</h3>
        $$A = \begin{bmatrix} -7 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 \end{bmatrix}$$
        <div class="proses">
            <p>Proses: \( \text{det}(A) = (-7 \times 4) - (-5 \times 1) \)</p>
            <p>\( \text{det}(A) = -28 + 5 \)</p>
            <p class="hasil">Hasil: \(\text{det}(A) = -23\)</p>
        </div>
    </div>

    <div class="soal">
        <h3>2. Matriks 3x3</h3>
        $$A = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}$$
        <div class="proses">
            <p>Ekspansi Baris ke-3:</p>
            <p>\( \text{det}(A) = 0(C_{31}) + 0(C_{32}) + 1 \begin{vmatrix} 0 & 2 \\ 1 & -2 \end{vmatrix} \)</p>
            <p>\( \text{det}(A) = 1 \times ((0 \times -2) - (2 \times 1)) \)</p>
            <p class="hasil">Hasil: \(\text{det}(A) = -2\)</p>
        </div>
    </div>

    <div class="soal">
        <h3>3. Matriks 4x4</h3>
        $$A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{bmatrix}$$
        <div class="proses">
            <p>Analisis: Jumlah elemen setiap baris adalah \(1 + (-3) + 1 + 1 = 0\). Berdasarkan sifat matriks, jika jumlah baris atau kolom adalah nol, maka matriks tersebut dependen linier.</p>
            <p class="hasil">Hasil: \(\text{det}(A) = 0\)</p>
        </div>
    </div>

    <h2>BAGIAN B: Invers Matriks (Metode Adjoin)</h2>

    <div class="soal">
        <h3>4. Invers Matriks Nomor 1</h3>
        <p>Menggunakan \( \text{det}(A) = -23 \)</p>
        <div class="proses">
            <p>Adjoin A: \( \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 \\ -1 & -7 \end{bmatrix} \)</p>
            <p>Rumus: \( A^{-1} = \frac{1}{\text{det}(A)} \text{adj}(A) \)</p>
            <p class="hasil">Hasil: \( A^{-1} = \begin{bmatrix} -4/23 & -5/23 \\ 1/23 & 7/23 \end{bmatrix} \)</p>
        </div>
    </div>

    <div class="soal">
        <h3>5. Invers Matriks Nomor 2</h3>
        <p>Menggunakan \( \text{det}(A) = -2 \)</p>
        <div class="proses">
            <p>Adjoin A (Transpose Kofaktor):</p>
            <p>\( \text{adj}(A) = \begin{bmatrix} -2 & -2 & -8 \\ -1 & 0 & -3 \\ 0 & 0 & -2 \end{bmatrix} \)</p>
            <p class="hasil">Hasil: \( A^{-1} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 4 \\ 0.5 & 0 & 1.5 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \)</p>
        </div>
    </div>

    <div class="soal">
        <h3>6. Invers Matriks Nomor 3</h3>
        <div class="proses">
            <p>Karena pada perhitungan nomor 3 nilai \( \text{det}(A) = 0 \), maka matriks ini bersifat <strong>Singular</strong>.</p>
            <p class="hasil">Hasil: Matriks tidak memiliki invers.</p>
        </div>
    </div>
